MISSION AND OUTREACH

The Mission and Outreach Committee of the Northern Plains Conference works with mission efforts in the different communities of the Conference. They also award grants to external projects. Currently the Mission and Outreach Committee is planning a mission trip. To Visit Mission and Outreach's Page

FAITH AND NURTURE

The Faith and Nurture Committee of the Northern Plains Conference works with the internal organizations of the Conference. These organizations include Church School, Youth Ministries and Women's Fellowship.

CHURCH AND MINISTRY

This Church and Ministry Committee is concerned with Ministerial Standing. New pastors entering the Northern Plains Conference go before this committee to seek standing in the Conference. The committee also handles the standing of local congregations.
